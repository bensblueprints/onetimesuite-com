FAQ

Fair questions

What makes it "viral"?

Two things: the price rises with every buyer, and a live public page shows the current price and recent buyers. People rush to buy before it goes up — and share it because the climbing number is fun to watch.

Where does the money go?

Straight into your own Stripe account. You connect Stripe once; Viral Invoice never holds your funds.

Is it really a one-time purchase?

Yes — $99 once for lifetime access through OneTime Suite (own the $997 Suite bundle and it is included free). Monthly plans for non-Suite users live at viralinvoice.com.

Do buyers get access automatically?

Yes. Every payment fires a webhook, emails the buyer an access link, unlocks a hosted access page, and can grant a Whop membership with their email pre-filled.

Can I stop it at a certain price?

Yes — set a price cap and the invoice sells out and closes itself the moment the next price would pass it.
