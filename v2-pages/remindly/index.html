How it works

Up and running in three steps

  1. 1

    Buy once on Whop

    One payment of $34 gets you the packaged Windows installer wired up, or clone the MIT source at github.com/bensblueprints/appointment-reminder-sms-mvp and run it free.

  2. 2

    Connect your Twilio and SMTP

    Drop your own Account SID, Auth Token and from-number into Settings, and add SMTP if you want email reminders.

  3. 3

    Import appointments and set rules

    Load appointments manually or by CSV, define reminder offsets, and let the scheduler send while clients reply C or R.

FAQ

Fair questions

Is it really free on GitHub?

Yes — Remindly is MIT-licensed at github.com/bensblueprints/appointment-reminder-sms-mvp. The $34 buys the packaged Windows installer with everything wired up; building from source is free.

Am I still paying for SMS?

Yes, but only the real carrier fee. Twilio charges ~$0.0079 per SMS on your own account and Remindly never marks it up — you skip the SaaS fee stacked on top, not the carrier cost.

Where does my client data live?

In your own SQLite file on your box, self-hosted with your own API keys — nothing goes to a third-party cloud.
