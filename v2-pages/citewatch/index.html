FAQ

Fair questions

Is it really free on GitHub?

Yes, Citewatch is MIT-licensed at github.com/bensblueprints/local-seo-citation-tracker-mvp. The $34 buys the packaged Windows installer and 1-click setup; building from source is free.

Where does my citation data live?

In a SQLite file on your own server, with no telemetry.

Does it crawl directories for me?

No, honestly. The MVP tracks the citations you audit by hand; nothing reliable crawls directories without expensive data deals, which is what you would be renting from BrightLocal. Google Business Profile can auto-pull with your own API key.
