# The Linktree Alternative That Doesn't Rent You Your Own Bio Link

Your link-in-bio page is the single most valuable URL you own. It sits in every profile, every video description, every email signature. So why rent it monthly from a company that keeps raising the price and putting the features you actually need behind the next tier up?

**LinkLeaf** is a free, open-source link-in-bio tool. Use the hosted version at [linkleaf.im](https://linkleaf.im) — claim your handle in 60 seconds, no credit card — or download the code and self-host it on your own domain. Either way, you own the page, the analytics, and the audience.

## Why people leave Linktree

- **The price keeps climbing.** Linktree raised Pro from about $9/mo to **$15/mo** in late 2025 ($12/mo if you pay annually). That's ~$180/year, forever, for a page of links.
- **The good stuff is gated.** Removing the Linktree logo, meaningful analytics, and click data all live behind the paid tiers.
- **They take a cut of your sales.** Linktree charges a commission on digital-product and tip transactions on top of your subscription.
- **Your data isn't really yours.** Analytics and subscriber capture live on Linktree's servers, on Linktree's terms.
- **Branding lock-in.** The free plan makes *you* advertise *them.*

## How LinkLeaf is different

- **Free, forever, on the hosted version** — claim a handle at linkleaf.im in about 60 seconds, no credit card, no trial clock.
- **Open source (MIT) and self-hostable** — the repo is Docker-ready, so you can run it on your own domain and infrastructure if you want full control.
- **Drag-to-reorder block editor** with a full catalog of block types — links, headings, embeds, and more.
- **6 polished themes plus custom styling** so your page looks designed, not templated.
- **Built-in analytics — views, clicks, and CTR — stored in your own database.** Not rented dashboards; your numbers.
- **No branding lock-in.** Your page promotes you, not us.
- **Fast, mobile-first public pages with zero trackers** — quick to load and privacy-respecting for your visitors.
- **Your subscriber data is always yours.**
