FAQ

Fair questions

Is it really free on GitHub?

Yes — the full source is MIT at github.com/bensblueprints/bg-remover-mvp. Paying $24 gets you the signed installer, 1-click setup and updates instead of building from source.

Is the quality as good as remove.bg?

It uses the same class of segmentation model (U²-Net family via ONNX). On typical product shots and portraits results are comparable; on very fine edges like wispy hair, remove.bg's newest cloud models can still win. You get unlimited retries for free, though.

Do my photos get uploaded?

Never. Inference runs on your CPU via onnxruntime. The only network call the app ever makes is the one-time model download.

Is it fast?

It runs on your own hardware, one image at a time by design (predictable memory). A batch takes a few seconds per image on a modern CPU — no queue, no credits, no per-image anxiety.

What formats are supported?

JPG, PNG and WebP in; transparent PNG or flat-color composites out, at the original resolution.
