- **It's a permanent subscription.** Flow Pro is **$15/mo, or $144/year** — and there is **no lifetime / buy-once option.** You rent it for as long as you use it.
- **The cost compounds.** ~$180/year means real money over the life of a tool you'll use daily for years.
- **Free tier has word limits.** Meaningful use pushes you onto Pro fast.
- **You're locked into their pricing roadmap.** Whatever they charge next year, you pay — or you lose the tool.

## How WisperTalk is different

- **$49 once — lifetime.** Buy it, own it. No monthly bill, no renewal, no annual creep.
- **Accurate transcription on Groq Whisper** — fast, high-quality speech-to-text.
- **AI cleanup with Llama 3.3** — turns raw dictation into polished, properly punctuated text, not a wall of run-ons.
- **Simple device licensing** — one device per license, with additional device licenses available for **$10 each** when you need them.
- **A true own-it-once tool** in a category that's gone almost entirely subscription.
