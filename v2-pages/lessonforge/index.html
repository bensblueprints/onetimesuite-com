How it works

Up and running in three steps

    Buy once on Whop

    One payment of $49 gets you the packaged Windows installer, or clone the MIT source at github.com/bensblueprints/course-builder-lms-mvp and run it yourself for free.

FAQ

Fair questions

Is it really free on GitHub?

Yes — Lessonforge is MIT-licensed at github.com/bensblueprints/course-builder-lms-mvp. The $49 buys the packaged installer and 1-click setup; building from source is free.

Where does my course data live?

In a SQLite file plus an uploads folder on your own server or machine. Nothing is uploaded to us.

Do you take a cut of my sales?

No. Payments go through your own Stripe account at Stripe's standard rates, and Lessonforge takes 0%. It's $49 once, no renewal.
