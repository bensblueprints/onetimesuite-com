FAQ

Fair questions

Is it really free on GitHub?

Yes — Postcraft Templates is MIT-licensed at github.com/bensblueprints/social-media-graphics-templates-mvp. The $19 buys the packaged Windows installer; building from source is free.

Is this a replacement for Canva?

No, and it's honest about that. Canva is a full design suite; Postcraft is a batch machine for repeatable branded posts. If you tweak every post by hand, keep Canva.

Does my content go to a cloud?

Never. It's 100% local — no accounts, no telemetry, and files land in a folder you pick.
