FAQ

Fair questions

Is it really free on GitHub?

Yes — Mockcraft is MIT-licensed at github.com/bensblueprints/mockup-generator-mvp. The $19 buys the packaged Windows installer; building from source is free.

Does it ship stock photos of devices and models?

No — that's the licensed imagery Placeit rents you. Every scene is honestly generated from vector shape specs by Mockcraft's own raster engine, so the whole pack is MIT-licensed with your copy.

Does anything leave my computer?

No. Mockcraft makes no network calls at all — your designs never leave your disk and exports go exactly where you choose.
